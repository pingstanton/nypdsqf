Education data from census tracts (2010, 2020 boundaries) were grouped into Precincts (defined by 2024 boundaries). In terms of inconsistencies between 2010 to 2020 census tracts, in most cases the change involved splitting the tract into two or more parts from a previous tract, not changing its associated NYPD Precinct. The major exception to this rule is found on Staten Island, where the 121st Precinct was created in 2013. Census tracts that were divided by boundries were grouped into the precinct of greatest area. For an illustrative guide, see: https://pingstanton.com/nypdsqf/mapCensusTractsPrecincts.htm
